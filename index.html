MY STORY

Who am I and what I do?

I like to express ideas with graphics to create clear, impactfull designs which engage and delight the viewer. My passions include typography, illustration, photography, coding, and web design. As a native of Russia. My education began at Siberia State University, studying transportation and logistics. But feeling enthusiastic about art, I decided to change my field to graphic design. I graduated Baruch College CUNY (City University of New York) with a bachelor’s degree in graphic communications concentrating in graphic and web design.
